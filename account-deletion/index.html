Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 18 February 2026

To request deletion of your Loop account and associated personal data, email us at support@loopappsupport.com from the email linked to your account.

What to include

  • Your account email address
  • Display name (if different)
  • "Delete my Loop account" in the message

What is deleted

  • Account/profile data
  • User-generated content tied to your account where applicable
  • Authentication access to the app account

What may be retained

  • Limited records required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes
  • Backups for a limited retention window

We aim to process verified requests promptly. If we need to verify identity, we may ask for additional confirmation.
