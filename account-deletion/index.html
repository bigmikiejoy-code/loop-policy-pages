Account Deletion

Last updated: 18 February 2026

If you would like to delete your Loop account and associated personal data, email us at support@loopappsupport.com from the email address linked to your account.

How to request deletion

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your account email address
  • Your display name, if different from your email
  • A clear request such as “Delete my Loop account”

What is deleted

  • Your account and profile data
  • User-generated content tied to your account, where applicable
  • Your authentication access to the app account

What may be retained

  • Limited records required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes
  • Backup data for a limited retention period

Processing and verification

We aim to process verified deletion requests promptly. If needed, we may ask for additional information to confirm your identity before completing the request.
