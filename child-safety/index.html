Loop Child Safety Standards

This page describes Loop’s standards and response process for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE/CSAM), including reporting and enforcement practices.

1) Zero tolerance policy

Loop has a zero tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation content or behavior. Any account, profile, message, image, or activity that appears to involve CSAE/CSAM is prohibited.

2) In-app reporting and moderation

  • Users can report inappropriate content in-app (including venue photo reporting).
  • Reported content is reviewed and can be removed.
  • Accounts that violate this policy may be suspended or permanently removed.

3) Escalation and legal compliance

Loop complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with relevant regional and national authorities when required.

4) Contact for child safety matters

Email: loop@loopappsupport.com

5) Scope

These standards apply to all users and all content submitted through the app, including text, profile details, and uploaded images.

Last updated: 2026-02-18

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services immediately.
