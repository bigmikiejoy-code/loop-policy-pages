Child Safety Standards

This page explains Loop’s standards and response process in relation to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE/CSAM), including reporting, moderation, and enforcement practices.

1. Zero tolerance policy

Loop has a zero tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation content or behaviour. Any account, profile, message, image, or activity that appears to involve CSAE/CSAM is strictly prohibited.

2. Reporting and moderation

  • Users can report inappropriate content and behaviour through in-app reporting tools.
  • Reported content may be reviewed, restricted, or removed where appropriate.
  • Accounts that violate this policy may be suspended or permanently removed.

3. Enforcement and legal compliance

Loop complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with relevant regional and national authorities where legally required.

4. Contact for child safety matters

If you need to report a child safety concern relating to Loop, contact:

Email: loop@loopappsupport.com

5. Scope

These standards apply to all users and all content submitted through the app, including profile information, text, messages, comments, and uploaded images.

Last updated: 18 February 2026

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services immediately.
